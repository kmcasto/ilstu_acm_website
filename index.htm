ACM at Illinois State

The ACM at Illinois State University is the local student chapter of the Association for Computing Machinery.

Group meeting announcments are made on our ACM Facebook page, as well as the joint ACM and AITP IT Club Facebook group.

SIGs

We have Special Interest Groups that focus on a variety of subjects, including game development, web, graphics, and mobile.

The SIGs meet at ACM group meetings as well as their own separate meetings which are posted on their respective facebook groups.

Recruiters & Internships

We work with recruiters to help match IT students with skills that employers are looking for. Each fall we host an IT specific career fair, for 2013 it was held on Sept. 25th.

Resume help and recruiter presentations are given during select AITP and ACM meetings, and will be announced on the ACM specific and joint IT Club facebook pages.

For general resume help and other information contact Tal Parmenter.
